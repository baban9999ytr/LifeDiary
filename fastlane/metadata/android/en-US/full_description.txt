Diary: The Freedom to Write

Diary is a local-first, open source Android diary app built on a single, uncompromising principle: no feature is forced on you. Whether you want to leverage all 20+ features or just focus on pure writing, the choice is yours.

Built by a 16-year-old developer from Bursa, Türkiye, this app is designed for those who value privacy, simplicity, and complete control over their personal data.
Total Privacy, No Compromises

    Offline-First: Your data never leaves your device. Ever.

    No Cloud: We don't store your words on any server.

    No Surveillance: No data collection, no trackers, and no ads.

    Open Source: Transparent by design. Check the code yourself.

Features You Can Use — If You Want:

    Rich Media: Attach photos, videos, and voice recordings to your entries.

    Personalization: Custom fonts, text colors, and cover photos.

    Creativity: A freehand canvas for drawing and sketching.

    Contextual Data: Automatic location, weather, and Google Fit step counts.

    Security: Lock and hide specific entries for your eyes only.

    Organization: Multi-account support and weekly statistics to track your progress.

    Motivation: Maintain daily writing streaks and view your growth.

Your diary, your rules.
